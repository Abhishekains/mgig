Why do we need sustainable infrastructure?

Sustainable infrastructure preserves, restores, and integrates the natural environment, including biodiversity and ecosystems. It supports the sustainable and efficient use of natural resources, including energy, water, and materials.

Sustainable infrastructure the way ahead for India

The fast-paced infrastructure development in India has not only helped the country in becoming one of the fastest growing economies of the world, but also aided in bringing in substantial investment.

Smart Cities Mission has given a boost to infrastructure development. With infrastructure geared towards Smart Cities, the focus is on sustainable infrastructure, which also becomes the need of the hour considering that the planet is suffering from eco-anxiety — due to climate change and diminishing natural resources.

We transform infrastructure in sustainable manner for better tomorrow by adopting three pillars of economic, environmental and social, which are informally referred as people, planet and profits.
